Genghis Khan

Genghis Khan, also known as Chinggis Khan, was the founder and first khagan of the Mongol Empire, which later became the largest contiguous land empire in history.

bio

  • Died: August 1227, Xingqing Prefecture

  • Spouse: Börte (m. ?–1227), Isukhan Khatun, Gurbasu Khatun, Gunju Khatun, Moge Khatun, Abika Khatun
  • Parents: Yesugei, Hoelun
